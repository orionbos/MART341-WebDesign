Biography

Orion Bos (b. 1999, he/him) is an artist and printmaker currently living and working in Eugene, Oregon. Born and raised in the Rocky Mountains of rural Montana, he is influenced by elements and relationships present in the rugged and ever-changing landscapes and ecosystems of home. In 2024, he received his BFA from the University of Montana where he collaborated with several visiting artists through Matrix Press in the creation of sizeable and varying editions of prints.
