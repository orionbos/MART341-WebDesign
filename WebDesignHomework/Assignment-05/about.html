Why I Chose These Ideas


Portfolio

I am an artist and a website to host images and information about me and my art/practice is extremely important. It's how people contact me, find me, buy art from me.


Archive/Blog

My art is very research based on invasive plant species. Because of this, I spend a lot of time bookmarking and going through field guides, plant books, and so on. I have many and each is used for different things. An archive site like this would allow me to consolidate and organize images, information, and more in a way that I could easily search for what I am after.


Marketplace/Shop

As an artist, if I want to keep being one, I must sell art. I currently use Squarespace, but if I create my own website outside of Squarespace I would need to host a marketplace for it too. This would be beneficial to me because Squarespace wouldn't be profiting off of my sales.
